Bicycle Sharing Service

HOW IT WORKS

A convenient solution for quick trips around Bhubaneswar City. The Bicycle Sharing system, developed by Mindfire, features thousands of bicycle at several stations across Bhubaneswar City. Bicycles are available 7:00 A.M to 10:00 P.M, 365 days a year. Station locations are based on population and transit needs, and were selected through a participatory public input process. Each station has a manager, a map of the service area and surrounding neighborhood, and a parking lot. You must be 15 or older to avail the service.

REGISTER

From Anywhere

Register with a nominal fee, provide any of your document as a proof, and you are ready to use the service from anywhere.

BOOK

From Anywhere

Come to any Pick-up Point or go Online for booking. Use your Id number and pay for the ride and your booking is confirmed.

RIDE

Wherever You Want

Take the bicycle and go anywhere but keep an eye on the clock. You will be charged for the service depending on the time you spent.

RETURN

To Any Pick-up Point

Whenever you are done go to any pick-up point and return the bicycle. Always remember you must return it before the end of the day.

RECHARGE

Your Wallet

Your trip charges are generally deducted from your wallet. Recharge your wallet before it runs out of balance. Do it from anywhere.

WHO WE ARE

"Bicycle Sharing" is Bhubaneswar's own bicycle sharing system. Intended to provide peoples of bhubaneswar and visitors with an additional transportation option for getting around the city, bicycle sharing is fun, efficient and convenient.

BICYCLE SHARING SYSTEM

A bicycle sharing system consists of a fleet of specially designed, sturdy, very durable bicycles that are placed in the parking lot sited at regular intervals around a city. The bicycles can be rented from and then returned to any station in the system, creating an efficient network with many possible points and combinations of departure and arrival.
